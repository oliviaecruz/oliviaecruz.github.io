about me

Soon to be image

I am a Boston based graphic designer with a love for branding, shapes and traditional crafts. I graduated from Boston University in 2013 with a BFA in Graphic Design.

Having a creative outlet started at a young age for me with days spent with my grandmother sewing, crocheting and working on other projects. Since then I’ve grow to love making amigurumi toys, designs and other crafts to give to friends and family (you can catch a glimpse of my Tigger and a golden snitch designs here). And am planning to open up an Etsy Shop soon so more people can get a hold on these cute little guys.

Whether a project is big or small, web or print, or anything else in between; I’m here to help you bring your project to life!
