ORNAMENTATION

inspiration: diy or die

Shown below is a book focusing on three types of ornamentation that influence me: yarn and thread, paper and fabric, and wood and metal. My love for this kind of detailed work began with my grandmother's lessons in crochet, embroidery, and sewing. I created the section openers, initial letters, and design elements for each of the sections, and curated the works shown in the book.

A hard cover book with belly band, 74 pages.
